WhatsHap is an instant messaging app that let's you communicate with people through their skin instead of their ears or eyes.

It may be useful to communicate with others haptically when visual or auditoriy modalities are inadequite. WhatsHap is used to render speech or text as haptic phonemes (speech sounds).

WhatsHap follows research by de Vargas et al in phoneme based haptic speech communication.

My role on this project:

  • Architecting IM system (key technologies: Node.js, Socket.io)
  • Running a user study
  • Quantiative and qualitative analysis
  • Technical paper writing
