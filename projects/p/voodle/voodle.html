Voodle: Vocal Doodling for Affective Robot Motion

Collaborators: Paul Bucci, Oliver Schneider, Karon MacLean

Generating believable robot motion is difficult, especially when those animations are meant to convey emotion. Emotion is a natural component of human speech, so we decided to use the speech signal as a basis to generate movement. By doing so, we ground affective representation in the speech signal itself, instead of a cognitive formalism internal to the robot.

We tested this system on a simple 1-degree-of-freedom robot, and created it through co-design, incorporating voice actors, artists, and audio engineers.
